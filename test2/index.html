In 2018, a small town in the Midwest

There were many people, those people are villagers in the town.

One day, there was a big storm above the sea.

Not many hours from then, tornadoes are formed by those storms.

The tornado moved on the land.

It hit the town, and the village collapsed.

Villagers were looking at those houses that collapsed.

They were talking together about how to overcome it.

Community members worked tirelessly to rebuild homes, businesses, and infrastructure.

Through their resilience and determination, the town was able to overcome the challenges and emerge stronger than ever before.
